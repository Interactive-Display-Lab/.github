## Facilities
Our lab is housed on the third floor of the state-of-the art $95M and 230,000 square foot Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at 306 N Wright St, Urbana, IL, room 3038. We are equipped with electronics assembly and debugging equipment, prototyping lab and optical bench, student offices, a photo and VR studio room, as well as a lounge and demonstration area. Departmental mechanical, electrical, and clean room fabrication facilities are located below on floors one and two.
